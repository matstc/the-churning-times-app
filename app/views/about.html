Who We Are

If you've spent any time at all reading our articles, you've probably realized that we are more influenced by e.e. cummings than any other writer.

Our purpose is to make churnalism really easy. Have a story you want to spin? Have a fact you want to create? Search no more. Head to our front page and join our staff of writers.

Churnalism

Churnalism is the act of passing anything as journalism. It's a great boon to PR departments and political committees. If you'd like to educate yourself in the art of churnalism, you can consult the following resources:

  • Churnalism.com
  • Churnalism at the Sunligh Foundation
